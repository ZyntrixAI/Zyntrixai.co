How It Works

Our Process

A clear, step-by-step process that keeps you informed and in control at every stage — from the first call to launch day and beyond.

01

Discovery & Brief

Every great website starts with a conversation. We kick off with a free 30-minute discovery call where we learn everything about your business — your goals, your target audience, your competitors, and what success looks like for you. Nothing is rushed. This session sets the strategic foundation for every decision that follows, ensuring the final site is built around your business, not just a template.

Free Consultation 30 Minutes No Commitment Required Competitor Research
What You Get

A detailed project brief document, a confirmed scope of work with transparent pricing, and an agreed delivery timeline — all signed off before any work begins.

02

Strategy & Design

Before a single line of code is written, we map out every page. First come low-fidelity wireframes — structural layouts that define the user journey, navigation flow, and information hierarchy. Once approved, we build full-colour visual mockups brought to life in your brand's palette, typography, and tone. You review and approve everything, with two full rounds of revisions included at no extra cost — we don't start building until you're genuinely happy with the design.

Wireframes + Full Mockups Desktop & Mobile Designs 2 Revision Rounds Included Brand-Consistent
What You Get

Complete visual designs for every page across desktop, tablet, and mobile, a style guide covering fonts, colours, and spacing, and Figma source files you own outright.

03

Development & Testing

With your designs signed off, we build — clean, semantic, well-structured code optimised for speed, security, and easy future maintenance. Everything is developed in a private staging environment first, so you can review the real, working site before anything goes public. We test across Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Performance is optimised to hit 90+ on Google Lighthouse, and accessibility standards are checked on every page. Nothing ships until it passes our full QA checklist.

Cross-Browser & Device Testing Mobile-First Development 90+ Lighthouse Target Private Staging Environment Accessibility Compliant
What You Get

A fully built, tested website on a staging URL for your review and sign-off, a Google Lighthouse performance report, and a full QA checklist confirming every feature has been verified.

04

Launch & Handover

Once every detail is approved, we handle the entire launch process — domain configuration, DNS management, hosting setup, SSL certificate activation, and Google Analytics 4 and Search Console installation. We monitor the live site closely in the first 24 hours to catch anything unexpected. After launch, 1–6 months of free support is included depending on your plan. You'll receive a full handover session so you know exactly how to manage your new site, and every file, credential, and piece of documentation is delivered directly to you. Your site. Your code. Your data.

Full Go-Live Support Analytics & Search Console SSL & Hosting Setup 1–6 Months Free Support Handover Session Included
What You Get

A live, fully indexed website, Google Analytics and Search Console configured from day one, a handover walkthrough, and complete delivery of all source files, login credentials, and project documentation.

Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

How long does it take to build my website?
Starter sites typically take 2–3 weeks. Growth projects take 4–6 weeks. Enterprise builds vary but we'll agree a full timeline before starting.
Do you require a deposit?
Yes — we ask for 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due on launch day. For larger projects we can split into milestone payments.
Can I update the website myself after launch?
Absolutely. We can build with a CMS (content management system) so you can edit text, images, and products yourself with no coding needed.
What if I need more pages than my package includes?
Additional pages are available from £80 each. We'll always confirm costs before doing any extra work so there are no surprises.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance after the free support period?
Yes — we offer monthly care plans from £79/mo covering updates, security monitoring, backups, and content changes. See our Services page for details.
