Our Story

THE STORY

Fringe Benefits opened its doors in 2018 with one chair, one barber, and an unwavering belief that a great haircut changes how a man carries himself. We built this place for the regulars — men who want more than a trim. They want a ritual.

Today we run three chairs with barbers who have trained under some of the best in the industry. Same philosophy, more seats. Walk-ins always welcome. Regulars always remembered.
