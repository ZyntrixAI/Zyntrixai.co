Our Story

Handcrafted
with Love

Every Beeutiful Scent candle is hand-poured with intention, one at a time, in our Castlemaine studio.

We use only the finest pure beeswax combined with certified organic essential oils — no synthetics, no shortcuts.

Each candle arrives in a stunning gold canister adorned with a signature brass bee. We believe in creating products that are kind to people and planet.

Founded with a passion for natural luxury, we're committed to sustainable practices, transparent sourcing, and the timeless art of candle-making.
