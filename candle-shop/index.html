Hand poured in Castlemaine

Organic fragrance, soft glow, and boutique finishing.

Beeutiful Scent candles are made with beeswax and organic essential oils, poured by hand in Castlemaine, Victoria. Each scent is finished in a gold canister with a brass bee detail, bringing warmth and a little luxury to everyday spaces.

The collection keeps the best of the original brand story: Australian made, rich with floral and citrus notes, and designed to feel elegant, grounded and memorable.

Origin

Crafted in Castlemaine, Victoria, Australia.

Scent profile

Bulgarian Rose, French Lavender, Lemon & Lime, and Vanilla blends in beeswax.

Brand story
