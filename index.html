About Beeutiful Scent

Founded in 2018, we create luxury candles that honor nature. Based in Castlemaine, Victoria, we're committed to sustainable practices.

Unlike synthetic waxes, beeswax burns cleaner, lasts longer, and naturally purifies the air. Each kg requires over 1 million flowers to produce.

Our Vision

We believe luxury doesn't have to harm the environment. Our mission is to create candles that are as beautiful to make as they are to burn.

We acknowledge the Dja Dja Wurrung and Taungurung peoples and honor their connection to Country. Every candle tells a story of flowers, oils, and intention.
