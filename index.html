About Beeutiful Scent

Handcrafted in Castlemaine, Victoria, Beeutiful Scent specializes in luxury beeswax candles infused with premium organic essential oils.

Each candle is carefully hand-poured using 100% pure beeswax, which burns cleaner and longer than traditional paraffin candles, while naturally purifying the air.

We're committed to sustainable, ethical practices and honor the Indigenous peoples of the lands on which we work.

🏡

Our Commitment

We acknowledge the Dja Dja Wurrung People of the lands on which we create our candles, with respect for their elders, knowledge, and ongoing connection to Country.

Every Beeutiful Scent candle is created with intention, combining the finest beeswax with premium organic essential oils to bring natural wellness and beauty to your home.
